Mission, Info, Plan

We are a self-formed club in TAS High School that provides multiple different chess experiences. We host outings,

events, competitions, tournaments, and fun club meetings year-round. We also compete in the IASIS tournaments for our school.

In the past 5 years, we have won more than 10 individual medals and club trophies for our school. It is a great honor and prestige

to represent and compete for TAS in regional competitions. We welcome everyone to join no matter their ratings. Sign up now!
